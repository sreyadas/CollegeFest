ABOUT KIIT FEST

KIIT FEST is a three day Annual techno-cultural college fest of KIIT University, Bhubaneswar. It is one of the largest Festivals of the Eastern paradise of India.KIITFEST is an ever-expanding universe of glory and greatness, where fun and games meet battles and battles become wars, where we put the sparkle in the air and glitz in the land and where we make the stars shine.
